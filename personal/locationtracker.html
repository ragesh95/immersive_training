With You - Location Tracker

Introduction

With you is mainly for sharing the location with your contact persons. With you will share the location whenever you switched on gps and internet. It does not interact with other apps and it only receive location from gps and all the permissions we acquired is mainly for the location sharing and for the user database. Users can share the location for their contact persons and history is stored in our database to provide users as a more features. Currently the app is only available for android mobiles and any version of android can make use of this application.
